We know at this moment, our community is facing a challenging downtime. If you have been recently layed off, and are seeking employment we hope that this webpage can help you get started and uplift our community. The City of San Antonio wants to let you know that we are in this together.

Gig Economy - a labor market characterized by the prevalence of short-term contracts or freelance work as opposed to permanent jobs. We know that during this time finding full-time employers is not an easy task. While you are job hunting, please consider looking into the curated list of gig-economy options we put together.

TaskRabbit

TaskRabbit is a platform that allows people to connect and hire people to perform tasks. Tasks can include building furniture, repair, to cleaning.

Visit San Antonio Marketplace

Upwork

Upwork is for people that have a proven creative talent(e.g. graphic illustrations), well developed technical skills(e.g. web development), and high quality freelancers. They have strict standards now and you need to have actual skills to be listed on their platform.

Visit their website

Instacart

Earn a little income by shopping and delivering groceries for others.

Visit their website
